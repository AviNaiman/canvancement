Quick Start

  1. Choose Configure API Settings and enter your Canvas Instance and your Access Token.
  2. Choose Specify Course and put in the Canvas Course ID for the course to analyze.
  3. Choose Load Due Dates to download all of the due dates from Canvas. Note that this will erase any existing data.
  4. Modify the dates
  5. Choose Save Due Dates to save your changes to Canvas.

If your times aren't correct, it may be a timezone issue. The spreadsheet's timezone settings are found under the File > Spreadsheet Settings menu item and should be set before you download the due dates.

The Reformat Dates menu item will reformat the dates in Canvas form. This is purely asthetic and is not necessary before you save the dates back to Canvas.

Quizzes are listed twice, once as a quiz and once as an assignment. You only need to change the dates on the quiz and it will automatically save them for the assignment.
